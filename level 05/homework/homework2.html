How I got into GOA

Let's talk a little about that story

I got into GOA thanks to my parents - my mom discovered it first, and my dad had already been thinking about trying out some courses related to computers, like programming. That got me curious too, so I decided to give it a try. Even though I still don't want to become just a programmer in the future, I believe it's important to understand this field, because it's going to be a big part of tomorrow's world.
